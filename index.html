Aerodynamic Properties Analysis in Star CCM+

This StarCCM+ simulation visualizes the total pressure coefficient distribution around a streamlined body, likely a Hatcback Car, immersed in a flow field. The color map ranges from red (high pressure) to blue (low pressure), with intermediate gradients indicating pressure variation across the surface and wake region. High-pressure zones are concentrated at the leading edge and stagnation points, while low-pressure areas trail along the sides and rear, revealing flow separation and wake dynamics. This analysis helps assess aerodynamic efficiency, drag behavior, and surface interaction, providing critical insights for design optimization and performance tuning.
