About Me

I am a Mechanical Design Engineer and CAD Specialist with 4 years of international experience in mechanical component and product development. I specialize in transforming engineering concepts into precise, manufacturable 3D models and technical drawings in accordance with ISO/DIN standards.

My expertise includes GD&T, tolerance analysis, design-for-manufacturing, and structured bills of materials. I work with leading CAD tools such as CATIA V5, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, and Autodesk Inventor, supported by experience in FEA simulations using ANSYS.

I have contributed to projects across different engineering domains, collaborating with interdisciplinary teams, suppliers, and production departments to deliver reliable and cost-efficient mechanical solutions. My approach combines technical accuracy, problem-solving mindset, and a strong focus on functionality and manufacturability.
